Wonkyung Do is a Ph.D. student in the ARMLab in Mechanical Engineering. He received his BS in Mechanical Engineering at the Seoul National University in 2019, and his MS in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University in 2022 (Advisor: Prof. Monroe Kennedy III). He worked at Naver Labs on developing online social touch pattern recognition using a multimodal tactile interface. He is interested in tactile sensing, control theory, optimization, human-robot interaction, and utilizing robotic perception systems to make the eidetic interface for an autonomous robot. https://arm.stanford.edu/people/wonkyung-do
